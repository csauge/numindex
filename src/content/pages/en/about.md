Welcome to **numindex.org**. Our mission is to promote joyful, conscious, and sustainable digital practices by indexing the best resources (tools, guides, associations, events) that share these values.

## Our Mission

Numindex was created to support the ecological transition in the tech sector through three main pillars:

* **Interactive Mapping:** We inventory actors, initiatives, and resources related to responsible digital technology to make them easily accessible to everyone.
* **Knowledge Sharing:** We spread best practices in ecodesign, accessibility, and ethics, helping every professional design more virtuous services.
* **Networking:** We are committed to connecting engaged organizations and changemakers to build a more sober digital ecosystem together.

## Our Commitment

In line with our values, the **numindex.org** platform itself applies the principles of digital sobriety:

* **Ecodesign and Performance:** We aim for a minimal carbon footprint through lightweight pages, strict media optimization, and the absence of unnecessary scripts.
* **Accessibility:** Our platform is designed to be accessible and inclusive, complying with web standards.
* **Transparency and Ethics:** The project is driven by a desire for openness, with eco-responsible hosting and minimalist data management.

## Quality and Eco-design

We automatically audit the performance and eco-design of our platform with every technical update. These audits are performed under strict conditions (average 4G mobile profile) to reflect the real user experience.
